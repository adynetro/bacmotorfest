Event Evolution

From Auto Meet to Motorfest

How a local auto meet evolved into a larger, more immersive festival format with broader impact.

Phase 1 — Auto Meet Vatra Dornei

The event started as Auto Meet Vatra Dornei, gathering modified and retro cars and building a strong local automotive community.

Phase 2 — Regional Growth

Participation expanded across multiple counties, with increased media presence, partnerships, and stronger audience engagement.

Phase 3 — Bucovina Auto Club Motorfest

In 2026, the concept evolved into a full festival identity: curated showcases, judged competitions, interactive audience activities, and stronger sponsor integration.

Phase 4 — Next Stage

Motorfest continues to scale as a premium automotive destination for enthusiasts, brands, creators, and visitors in Bucovina.
