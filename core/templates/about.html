About the Event

Bucovina Auto Club Motorfest

A modern auto festival focused on modified builds, retro icons, motorsport culture, and memorable community experiences.

What Motorfest Is

Bucovina Auto Club Motorfest is the next chapter of the Auto Meet Vatra Dornei initiative. It brings together modified cars, retro classics, creators, brands, and automotive fans in one premium event format.

The festival combines curated car displays, judged competitions, social experiences, and strong visual storytelling to create a complete destination event in the heart of Bucovina.
